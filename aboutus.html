SOME OVERVIEW ABOUT OUR WORK!



Al Arif Tea Store came into being in 1983 in Karachi by Muhammad Hassan, had a vision of introducing a unique and fresh taste of tea for the tea-lovers of Pakistan and providing them perfect blends to fulfill different taste buds. But over time, our tea business grew and flourished as many customers liked and praised the quality and taste of our tea. The secret of our success lies way back from the beginning where we have collaborated with top tea producers and suppliers of the world to bring the richness of tea to your cups. Al Arif Tea Store imports tea from Kenya, Sri Lanka, Rwanda, China, and Vietnam from their best tea farms where the finest quality of tea plants are grown and each tea leave is picked with care and love. A wide number of our business partners are our distributors who are operating Round the Clock to bring Standard Tea products in the market. They all are the prime service providers and extremely professionals in Tea Business. Together our business partners and our team of professionals, makes it possible to bring the best brands with a combined effort of dedication and enthusiasm. Here at Standard Tea house, we have a well-trained team of more than 200 employees working in their specialized fields, more than 5 best tea exporters of the world, and a strong channel of Supply Chain to manufacture and deliver the Standard Tea brands which you all love. With the experience of the past 30 years, Al Arif Tea Store has become a renown tea brand of Pakistan extending its products from Mixture, Danedar, Dust and Green Tea leaves. At Standard Tea, we use modern logistics, automatic machines, and a strong retail system to provide our customers with freshly packed teas every time. Al Arif Tea Store is dedicated to providing safe, pure and healthy tea products that meet the high expectations of our customers and consumers
